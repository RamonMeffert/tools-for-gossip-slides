         Number relation & $N \subseteq A \times A$\\\pause
         Secret relation & $S \subseteq A \times A$\\\pause
         Gossip graph    & $G = (A, N, S)$\\\pause
         Call            & $ab$\\\pause
         Identity relation & $I_A = \{\ (x, x) \mid x \in A\ \}$\\
